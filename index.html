Our Menu

Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian meat sauce that is a staple in most families. It's made with ground beef, onions, garlic, crushed tomatoes, and a few simple seasonings.

Chicken Shawarma

Chicken Shawarma is a Middle Eastern chicken recipe that is made with marinated chicken, garlic sauce, and pita bread. It is a popular street food in the Middle East and is often served with a side of hummus or tabbouleh.

Beef Burger

Beef Burger is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. The patty may be pan fried, grilled, smoked or flame broiled.

Chicken Salad

Chicken Salad is a salad that typically consists of shredded or diced chicken, mixed with mayonnaise and other ingredients such as celery, hard-boiled eggs, and seasonings.
